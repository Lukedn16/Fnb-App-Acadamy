PANDAS

Giant pandas (Ailuropoda melanoleuca) are native to the mountain forests of central China. They are known for their distinctive black-and-white fur and gentle nature. Pandas primarily eat bamboo, which makes up more than 99% of their diet.

Pandas are solitary animals and spend most of their day eating or sleeping. They are a conservation symbol worldwide and efforts to protect their habitats have helped stabilize their population in the wild.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM

You will find pandas at pen 16, near the bamboo forest habitat.
